Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Widely referred to as the "People's President", he was highly respected and loved by all. Dr. Kalam was instrumental in India's civilian space program and military missile development, thus earning the title "Missile Man of India".
